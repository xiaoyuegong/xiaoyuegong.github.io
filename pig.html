A Story About Loneliness

A Story About Loneliness
This is a work of Stop Motion animation I made with a pig puppet. I composed and produced the music pieces for this story about loneliness. The animation tells how I understood loneliness. There were a bunch of questions that can be asked regarding what happened in this story and everyone can have his/her own interpretation

A lot of the items used in composing the animation have special meaning to me. For example, the little book that the protagonist pig reads to the piggie on the whiteboard is a book I took from a missile shelter when I was studying in Israel at a time of war.
