Land of Courtesy 礼仪之邦

Land of Courtesy 礼仪之邦
This is a piece of Chinese wide-sleeves dance about Chinese traditional values of courtesy.
The filming of our performance was at the send-off ceremony for the inaugural class of NYU Shanghai at the feet of the Oriental Pearl TV Tower in Shanghai, China in late May, 2017.

The music is edited from Land of Courtesy 礼仪之邦 by 安九、璿璣.
The performers are members of the inaugural class of NYU Shanghai (in alphabetical order):
Xiaoyue Gong, Yuting Gong, Yuan Liu, Yuxin Tong, Yu Zhou.
